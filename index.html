Welcome to Linfield International College

Linfield International College, established in January 2005, is a fully equipped international school situated only 2km away from the city of Maharagama providing education to students from the lower nursery level to pre grade. At Linfield our medium of instruction is English and Sinhala is taught as a second language and we offer a variety of subjects along with extracurricular activities in order to develop each student into becoming a fully fledged scholar in the future
